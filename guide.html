Basktics – User Guide

Step-by-step instructions

⬅ Back to Home

1. Download the app

⬇ Download

2. Unzip the file

Enter team names and start the game.

3. Click Setup

Inside the extracted folder, you will find the setup file. Click on it.

Do not worry about the warning messages. Proceed normally and click ‘Install’

4. Choose Match or Players

The ‘Match’ section is for having all the player statistics. In ‘Players’, you simply enter some details for individual training sessions.

All data will be saved in Excel files.

5. Match Section

  • First enter the players in the lower table. The first five players are automatically set as the starting lineup. By entering the positions, they are also automatically added to the ‘Points from Position’ table.
  • Click ‘Start’ to begin the timer. During time-outs and at the end of periods, click ‘Pause’.
  • In the ‘Substitutions’ table, you can make substitutions, and the playing time of each player who leaves is updated automatically.
